About me

Precision design, real-world impact

I'm MD Touhidur Rohman, a 2nd year Mechanical Engineering student at Khulna University of Engineering & Technology (KUET), Bangladesh. I have a strong passion for 3D CAD modeling and product design using SolidWorks. My work spans multiple engineering domains — from UAV frame design and agricultural machinery to precision mechanisms and fluid-dynamic components. I'm also beginning my journey in simulation with ANSYS, exploring how computational tools can validate and improve mechanical designs before they're built. I believe great engineering is the intersection of aesthetics and function — where every curve, edge, and constraint serves a purpose.
